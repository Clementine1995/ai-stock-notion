---
name: risk-checker
description: Identify common short-term trading risks in events and reports.
stage: m5
---

Use this skill before finalizing any observation or report section.

Check for:
- Good news already priced in at a high level.
- Weak sector confirmation.
- Ambiguous or low-quality evidence.
- Regulatory, dilution, unlock, or shareholder reduction risk.
- Conclusions without an invalidation condition.

Output a concise risk note and a risk_score from 1 to 5.
